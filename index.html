About Zouq Al-Khamis

Nestled in the heart of Khamis Mushait, our restaurant brings together the rich flavors of Pakistani, Arabic, and Asian cuisine. From sizzling kebabs to fragrant biryanis and hearty curries, every dish is made with authentic spices and fresh ingredients.

With a warm, welcoming atmosphere and options for both indoor and outdoor dining, it’s the perfect spot for family gatherings, casual lunches, or festive dinners. Each meal is crafted to offer not just great taste, but a memorable dining experience.
